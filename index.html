Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this site is to show HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap skills developed as a part of the University of Minnesota's Data Analytics Bootcamp.

The analysis of the information on this site is to provide visualizations and descriptions for what happens to weather as you get closer to the equator.


We assembled the dataset, of 500 or greater cities, using OpenWeatherMap's API.

We used Matplotlib to do create vizualizations of various weather conditions to describe trends and patterns we noticed with the graphs we created.
